About me

Hello there! I'm deeply passionate about mathematics, logic, and programming. Since childhood, I've been fascinated by the challenge of creating and solving puzzles. Despite my involvement in high-level handball for over a decade, my interest in innovation has always burned brightly.

My journey into programming commenced in 2019 when I enrolled at TEC. It was a eureka moment—I found myself quickly grasping concepts and delving into the intricacies of coding. Now, I'm eagerly seeking an apprenticeship that will allow me to further cultivate my skills and expand my knowledge in this dynamic field.

Looking forward, I envision myself making meaningful contributions within a respected organization, specializing in game, app, or website development. Collaboration is at the heart of my approach, as I thrive in environments where diverse perspectives converge to drive innovation. As I approach graduation in 2025, I'm excited to embrace new challenges and continue my journey of growth and discovery in the world of programming.

Ultimately, my goal is to leverage my skills and passion to make a meaningful impact in the realm of game development. Programming isn't just a career path for me—it's a lifelong passion driven by curiosity, creativity, and the desire to create something remarkable.
